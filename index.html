About

Mr. Cakes is a phenomenal cake baker based in Hudsonville, Michigan and caters to local birthdays and parties. His cakes, baked using recipes he's honed to delicious perfection, enjoy an amazing reputation for excellence and he's never had a displeased customer. He offers a large variety of simple, yet gorgeous designs, everything from peanut butter chochlate, to just plain chochlate and vanilla. His cakes are easily affordable at an average of $20 a cake. You can't go wrong buying a cake from Mr. Cakes!

In his spare time, Mr. Cakes enjoys minecraft, basketball, football, airsoft, and eating his baked masterpieces.
